🔍 What happens during proof generation?

COMPUTED LOCALLY (private)
  • Recompute leaf hash from birth year + secret + nonce
  • Walk Merkle tree using siblings + path indices
  • Verify computed root matches public root
  • Check birth_year + threshold ≤ current_year
  • Bind proof to verifier's session nonce
NEVER SHARED WITH VERIFIER
  • Your birth year
  • Your citizen secret
  • Your nonce issuance
  • Your leaf index in the tree
  • Your Merkle path siblings
  • Your name or any personal data
